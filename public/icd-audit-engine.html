What is an ICD Audit Engine?

An ICD Audit Engine is not a simple search tool. It is an autonomous logic system designed to validate medical documentation against thousands of complex ICD-10-CM guidelines in real-time.

Unlike traditional "Computer Assisted Coding" (CAC) which relies on keyword matching, our engine understands the clinical relationships between diseases. It knows that Sepsis requires an underlying infection code. It knows that Diabetes and CKD are presumed linked unless stated otherwise. It mimics the reasoning of a Senior Medical Auditor.

Why We Built It

The manual coding model is broken.

Human Error

Even the best coders have a 95% accuracy ceiling. Fatigue, complex charts, and changing guidelines lead to unavoidable mistakes.

Claim Rejections

Simple sequencing errors or unspecified codes cause millions in lost revenue and administrative rework costs.

Compliance Risks

Under-coding leads to revenue loss. Over-coding leads to OIG audits. Our engine keeps you perfectly in the safe zone.
