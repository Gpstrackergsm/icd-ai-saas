DEFINITION

What is Smart ICD-10?

Smart ICD-10 is a next-generation clinical coding and audit system built on deterministic AI. Unlike simple lookup tools, it simulates the decision-making process of a senior medical coder. It parses natural language clinical stories, identifies distinct medical conditions, applies official inclusion/exclusion rules, and generates a fully validated, compliant list of ICD-10-CM codes.

Why Smart ICD-10 is Different

Beyond keyword matching. We built a semantic clinical engine.

Clinical Context

Sequencing Validation

Enforces mandatory "Code First" and "Use Additional" rules. Ensures etiology is coded before manifestation.

Rule-Based Intelligence

No hallucinations. Our outcomes are deterministic and based strictly on the ICD-10-CM tabular list and guidelines.

Common Questions

What is Smart ICD-10?

Smart ICD-10 is our proprietary AI core designed to read medical documentation and output audit-grade ICD-10-CM codes, fully compliant with US healthcare standards.

Is this official?

The engine bases all decisions on the Official Guidelines for Coding and Reporting (FY 2025). It is a technological implementation of official rules.

Is it a replacement for coders?

No. It is a productivity force-multiplier. It acts as a safety net and an assistant, handling the heavy lifting of code search and rule verification.

Can it replace manuals?

While it contains the intelligence of the manuals, professional coders should always have access to official text. Smart ICD-10 serves as an expert interactive manual.

Does it handle complex cases?

Yes. It specializes in multi-comorbidity cases like Sepsis with Organ Failure, Diabetic complications, and Traumatic injuries requiring 7th character specificity.
