Pregnancy & Childbirth (Category O00-O9A)

Coding for obstetrics requires identifying the specific condition and the trimester. Z3A codes, indicating weeks of gestation, are typically required as additional codes.

Code Description
O24.419 Gestational diabetes mellitus in pregnancy, unspecified control
O80 Encounter for full-term uncomplicated delivery
Z3A.xx Weeks of gestation (Required for all O codes)
